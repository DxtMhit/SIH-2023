State-wise Dropout Analysis

State-by-state dropout analysis is a technique for assessing and comprehending dropout rates in educational institutions or programmes across many states or regions. It entails looking at the number of students who drop out before finishing their education and detecting trends, causes, and discrepancies between states. Economic factors, educational policy, and societal demography can all have an impact on dropout rates. This research assists policymakers and educators in developing targeted interventions and strategies to minimise dropouts and enhance educational outcomes, ensuring that kids in all states have equitable access to excellent education and opportunity for success.
